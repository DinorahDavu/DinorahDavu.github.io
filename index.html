Collection Quick Links

  • Ebook Central (formerly Ebrary)
    Multidisciplinary, Children’s and Teen Literature
    Complete access to over 170,000 title spanning various disciplines. Use Adobe Digital Editions to download. Use Bluefire reader for downloading to mobile devices.
  • EBSCO Ebook Collection
    Multidisciplinary
    Search over 200,000 titles across all academic subject areas.
  • Oxford University Press E-Book Collection
    Multidisciplinary
    Access to over 16,000 academic eBooks from Oxford University Press (OUP) on their University Press Scholarship Online platform.
  • SAGE Knowledge
    Access ebooks and e-reference resources across multiple subject areas.
  • JStor
    Multidisciplinary
    Some full-text of journals, books, historical documents, letters, and interviews. Includes images and 3-D models. Most recent 2-5 years not included.

Collections

The JKM library has an extensive collection of eBooks accessible through the library's catalog and databases. These collections cover a variety of subjects including the humanities, arts, sciences, history, engineering, religion, health sciences and more. Use the tabs above to access the various collections and learn the details on searching and accessing eBooks.
